Creamy Spinach Pizza

Home

Description

Creamy spinach pizza is a pizza with spinach for toppings and creamy cheese to give it flavor. Since it has no meat, it's a great option for vegeterians.

Ingredients

  • Dough
  • Cheese
  • Spinach
  • Tomato sauce

Steps

  1. Knead dough into shape.
  2. Add tomato sauce, toppings, and cheese, in that order.
  3. Leave it in the oven for 15-30 minutes depending on heat intensity.
